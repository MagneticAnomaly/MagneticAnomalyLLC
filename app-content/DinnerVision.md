**DinnerVision** is an intelligent mobile app designed to eliminate the daily struggle of deciding what's for dinner. By harnessing the power of computer vision and AI, DinnerVision instantly transforms the random ingredients in your fridge into delicious, actionable meal ideas.

## The Problem We Solve
We've all been there: staring into the fridge at 6:00 PM, overwhelmed by decision fatigue, and tempted to just order takeout. Meal planning is tedious, and perfectly good food often goes to waste simply because we don't know what to do with it.

DinnerVision solves this by bridging the gap between **what you own** and **what you can cook**.

## How It Works

1. **Snap a Photo:** Simply point your camera at your ingredients.
2. **Instant AI Recognition:** DinnerVision's computer vision instantly identifies what you have on hand.
3. **Smart Suggestions:** Get tailored, high-quality meal suggestions that utilize your fresh ingredients alongside assumed pantry staples.
4. **Cook & Enjoy:** Pick a suggestion that fits your mood and get a complete, step-by-step recipe.

## Key Features

- **Camera-First Meal Ideas:** No more tedious typing. Just scan and see what's possible.
- **Reduce Food Waste:** The app's smart inventory system gently nudges you to use fresh ingredients before they expire.
- **Complete Control:** Filter suggestions by cuisine, available time, dietary preferences, and complexity to find exactly what you're craving.
- **Quality Recipes:** Say goodbye to disappointing AI hallucinations. DinnerVision generates reliable, easy-to-follow recipes based on verified culinary principles.
- **Smart Pantry Assumptions:** Whether your pantry is "Running on Empty" or "Fully Stocked," the app adjusts its recommendations to match your real-world kitchen setup.

## Why DinnerVision?
- **Save Time:** Cut out the heavy lifting of meal planning. Your time-to-first-suggestion is under 30 seconds.
- **Save Money:** Use up the groceries you already bought instead of letting them spoil.
- **Discover Variety:** Break out of your cooking rut with creative suggestions you might not have thought of on your own.
